Our Message:

Plant the joy of outdoor gardening in your kids and raise up a generation of botanists.

A little more about our stance

We are a nonprofit organization that is looking to give back to the environment by teaching people from a young age how to treat our earth. Taking care of the planet is our resposability and the more you know the better you are able to help fight the problems.

A look into the programs we offer

We offer educational type classes where we go into more debth on how you can help the environment by simply changing how you treat the plants around your home. If you are interested in enrolling in a program please see contact page. Some of our up coming classes include:

    An Introduction to Raising Vegetables (August 17-23)

    An Introduction to Herb Treatment (August 23-September 5)

    An Introduction to Vegetable Growing (September 8-15)
